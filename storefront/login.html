We formulate strictly according to ancient Ayurvedic texts, using pure cold-pressed botanical oils, sun-dried herbs, and floral extracts. Our products are completely free from parabens, sulfates, phthalates, mineral oils, and artificial fragrances.

How often should I use the Bhringraj & Amla Hair Oil? +

For optimal results, we recommend gently warming the oil and massaging it into your scalp 2-3 times a week. Leaving it on overnight allows the active botanical nutrients to deeply penetrate the hair follicles.

Is the Soundarya Ubtan suitable for sensitive skin? +

Yes, absolutely. Our ubtan is crafted with soothing ingredients like pure sandalwood powder and fresh rose petals. If you have sensitive skin, we recommend mixing it with pure rose water or raw milk rather than plain water or yogurt.
